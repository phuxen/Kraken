Bollard

Bollard is a fat and cuddly sans serif typeface which was created during typographic experiments with simple shapes and elements. It works best at sizes over 72pt and with simple contrasting colours.

Unremitting

In the process of creating the squiggles typeface, this was an early prototype for experimentations creating joined up letterforms. It has a series of connecting rods that reach up or down to the line above or below, thus enabling to connect multiple lines of text.

Who is Kraken?

Kraken is run by the bearded & bespectacled Paul Huxen who, when not making fonts, will be found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town.

Why call it “Kraken”?

I get this a lot, basically I was doodling away in my Moleskine and drew a ‘K’,‘R’ & ‘N’ and thought to myself ‘What begins with those letters?’ A quick little think and Kraken appeared. It's lucky that I think a Kraken is awesome too. It could of almost of been called ‘Narwhal’…

How do I say hello?

Just throw a nice little email to hello@krakenfoundry.co.uk
